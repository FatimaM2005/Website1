Purpose and the existential

Purpose can guide life decisions, influence behaviour, shape goals, offer a sense of direction, and create meaning. For some people, purpose is connected to meaningful work. For others, their purpose lies in their responsibilities to their family or friends, however the most significant source of purpose is serving the all mighty, all merciful, omnipresent who is of course god. After all, things like work, riches, family and legacy are important but they will eventually vanish with time. This poses an important question, what is the point of doing anything at all. Allah answers this in chapter 4 verse number 98 of the Quran which can be explained as the following; "I have only created Jinns and men, to serve Me." Servitude to a higher infinite being, is what makes life worth living. This coupled with the knowledge of life after death, heaven and hell, and a day of judgement is the fuel one needs to go through life with a strong foundation. A servant of the creator, frees one from the creation. It frees one from the slavery to money, people and whims and desires instead allowing one to devote oneself to all that is good and pure. Purpose and spirituality is essential for a successful and strong mind.

Gratitiude

In positive psychology research, gratitude is strongly and consistently associated with greater happiness. Gratitude helps people feel more positive emotions, relish good experiences, improve their health, deal with adversity, and build strong relationships.

02

Contentment

contentment causes man to suffice to the amount he needs and to avoid craving for more. In fact, contentment is a feeling of calm and active happiness. Somebody who is content does not feel the need to put forth any effort into achieving something because he or she is satisfied with his portion.

03

Honesty
